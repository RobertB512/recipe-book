Description

Ingredients

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • cornmeal

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (218 degrees C). In a medium bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in flour, salt and oil. Beat until smooth. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round. Transfer crust to a pizza pan or baker's peel with parchment paper, dusted with cornmeal.
  4. With a fork, gently poke wholes multiple times throughout dough. Lightly brush dough with oil. Bake for 5 minutes on middle rack.
  5. Remove from oven and spread with desired toppings and bake another 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
